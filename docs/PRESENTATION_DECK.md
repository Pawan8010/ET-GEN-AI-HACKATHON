# OpsBrain AI — Presentation Deck Source

## Slide 1 — One operational brain

**OpsBrain AI**

Turn scattered industrial knowledge into confident action.

Procedures, assets, incidents, inspections, regulations, and maintenance history connected by grounded AI.

## Slide 2 — The operational problem

Critical evidence is fragmented across documents, handovers, maintenance records, and compliance files. Teams lose time searching, repeat failures, and cannot easily prove why a decision was made.

## Slide 3 — The product answer

OpsBrain creates one searchable operational memory. Upload evidence once; the platform extracts, chunks, embeds, connects, retrieves, and cites it automatically.

## Slide 4 — How the system works

`Upload → Extract → Chunk → Embed → Index → Graph → Retrieve → Ground → Cite`

Use the architecture diagram from `docs/ARCHITECTURE_DIAGRAM.md` as the visual.

## Slide 5 — Trust at the point of work

Ask: “What caused the near-miss involving PMP-101?”

Show the cited incident, the isolation SOP, related VLV-204 entity, retrieval diagnostics, and confidence explanation. Emphasize that unsupported questions return “not found” instead of invented safety guidance.

## Slide 6 — Beyond chat

Asset Memory, Graph Intelligence, RCA, Compliance AI, Lessons Learned, and live Insights share the same evidence layer. A finding in one module becomes useful everywhere else.

## Slide 7 — Demonstrable impact

Faster troubleshooting, fewer repeated incidents, clearer audit evidence, and a durable scale path to PostgreSQL, Qdrant, Neo4j, Redis, object storage, and workers.

## Slide 8 — Close

OpsBrain makes industrial knowledge connected, searchable, explainable, and ready before the next decision matters.
