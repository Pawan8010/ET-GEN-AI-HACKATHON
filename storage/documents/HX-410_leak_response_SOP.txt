HX-410 Heat Exchanger Leak Response SOP
Document status: DEMONSTRATION DATA for prototype validation

If hydrocarbon is detected in the cooling-water return from heat exchanger HX-410, stop the affected train according to the approved shutdown sequence, isolate process and utility sides, notify the control room, and collect a sample under the chemical exposure procedure. Do not open the channel head until pressure and temperature are confirmed zero.

The incident owner must preserve the first sample, record time and location, inspect tube-sheet evidence, and link the event to HX-410 and the relevant work order. A restart requires leak-boundary verification, an independent permit review, and a signed operations handover.
